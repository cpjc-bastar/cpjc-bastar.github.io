About the website

Bastar has endured decades of violence, state repression, and human rights violations, marked by brutal actions including extrajudicial killings, arbitrary detentions, torture, and severe suppression of dissent. The situation has only worsened recently. Despite the gravity and persistence of these violations, information about these incidents remains scattered and fragmented, making it difficult to access and understand the full extent of the ongoing crisis.

This platform seeks to address this gap by consolidating publicly available information, reports, legal documents, news coverage, statements, and multimedia resources in one place.
